Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about Enchanted Hub, our scripts, and how to get support.

What is Enchanted Hub?

Enchanted Hub is a platform that provides high-quality scripts for Roblox games, specifically focusing on Legends of Speed and Muscle Legends. Our scripts are designed to enhance your gaming experience and help you progress faster.

Are these scripts safe to use?

Yes, all our scripts are thoroughly tested to ensure they're safe to use. However, as with any script, there's always a small risk of detection. We recommend using a reliable executor and not abusing the scripts to minimize any risks.

How do I use the scripts?

To use our scripts, you'll need a Roblox script executor. Copy the loadstring from our Scripts page, paste it into your executor, and then execute it while in the game. The script will automatically load and provide you with a user interface to control its features.

Which games do you support?

Currently, we support Legends of Speed and Muscle Legends. We're constantly working on expanding our script library to include more popular Roblox games. Stay tuned for updates!

How often are scripts updated?

We update our scripts regularly to ensure compatibility with game updates. Major updates are usually announced on our Discord server, so make sure to join to stay informed about the latest changes.

How can I get support?

If you need help with our scripts or have any questions, the best way to get support is through our Discord server. We have a dedicated support channel where our team and community members can assist you with any issues you might encounter.

Need more help?

If you couldn't find the answer to your question, feel free to reach out to us on Discord.
